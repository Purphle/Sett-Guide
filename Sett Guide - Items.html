A Guide for Sett

General Runes Items Gameplay

Sett - The Boss | Runes


  This page will overgo the most optimal choices for Sett items.
  With the recent nerfs to Stridebreaker - you always want to rush Goredrinker.
  Always pick defensive boots.


Starter Items:

Doran's Blade:

 
8 attack damage

80 health

2.5% Omnivamp
  • Gives you damage and omnivamp
  • Take into winning matchups
  • Never take into ranged champions

Doran's Shield:

 
80 Health

Restores 6 HP per 5 second
  • Gives you HP regen upon taking champion damage
  • Take into losing matchups and ranged champions

Health Potion:

 
Regenerates 5 HP every 0,5 seconds

Lasts 15 seconds

Restores 150 health
  • It's a health potion
  • You'll need it
  • Use after a loss of HP
  • Self-explanatory


Core Items:

Goredrinker:

 
Deals AD damage around you

Heals for 25% AD and 10% missing HP
  • The only good remaining mythic item on Sett
  • Activate only while around 3 or more champions OR near death

Sterak's Gage:

 
Gain a shield after dropping to 30% of your max HP

Shield lasts for 4 seconds
  • Great against burst

Black Cleaver:

 
Carve - a stack that reduces enemy's armor by 5%

Max 6 stacks - 30% armor reduction
  • Great against heavy armor comp
  • Can be used as an alternative to Sterak's Gage - in case
    they have Serpent's Fang

Titanic Hydra:

 
Gain bonus attack damage equal to 2% max HP

Auto-attacking deals damage in a cone behind the target
  • Offers attack damage and waveclear


Situational Items:

Hullbreaker:

 
While not in range of ally champions:

gain bonus armor and magic resist

deal bonus damage to enemy structures
  • Only take if you're the only one ahead
    and your team sucks

Chempunk Chainsword:

 
Dealing damage to enemy inflics Grievous Wounds for 3 seconds
  • Anti-healing
  • Offers damage
  • Reduces opponent's healing and HP regeneration by 40% - 60%

Thornmail:

 
Taking damage from enemies, deals back magic damage

Inflicts Grievous Wounds
  • Anti-healing
  • Offers armor
  • Reduces opponent's healing and HP regeneration by 40% - 60%

Spirit Visage:

 
Increases all regeneration and shielding by 25%

40 magic resist
  • Against heavy AP teams
  • Gives you faster regeneration

Silvermere Dawn:

 
Removes all crowd control effects - aside from airborne

Grants extra tenacity, slow resist and ghosting for 3 seconds
  • Useful against Mordekaiser and Malzahar
