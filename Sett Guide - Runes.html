General Runes Items Gameplay

Sett - The Boss | Runes


  This page will overgo the most optimal choices for Sett runes.
  In short - the main rune will be:
  Conqueror - for melee matchups
  Fleet Footwork - for ranged matchups
  As for secondary tree - you'll be using Resolve.


Keystones:

Conqueror:

 
  • Mostly picked for melee, can be used for ranged too
  • Gives health and damage for extended fights
  • Scales great

Fleet Footwork:

 
  • Only pick into ranged matchups
  • Offers sustain for harder laning phases

Minor runes:

 

Triumph:

  • The most optimal rune out of the 3
  • The other 2 don't bring much value
  • Can help you survive 2v1 situations
  • Brings extra gold upon takedowns
  • No reason not to pick Triumph

Legend - Alacrity:

 
  • First option for secondary minor rune
  • Offers attack speed
  • Good for fighting
  • Bad against crowd control

Legend - Tenacity:

 
  • Second option for secondary minor rune
  • Offers tenacity
  • Good against heavy CC team
  • Bring into passive lanes

Last Stand:

 
  • Gives you extra damage when low on health
  • You'll be getting low on HP often
  • Can help you survive when low
  • Coup de Grace doesn't offer the same amount of value



Secondary Tree:

Resolve:


Bone Plating:

 
  • Good into melee matchups
  • Lowers the damage of the next first 3 attacks
  • Take into bursty melees

Second Wind:

 
  • Can take it into both melee and ranged matchups
  • Works great with his W and passive

Revitalize:

 
  • Best out of the 3 minor runes
  • Works great with his W and passive
  • Take it most of the time

Unflinching:

 
  • Only take into heavy CC teams
  • Combine it with Legend: Tenacity
