Sett - The Boss


  Sett, ever since his release, has been a powerful pick. He's been picked by both casuals.
  and pros for good reasons.
  This guide will run over everything you need to know about Sett and how to win with him.
  First I'll go over the pros and cons, abilities and then run over runes and items.




Pros

  • Strong Damage
  • High Health
  • Easy to Pick Up and Learn
  • Total Lane Bully
  • Can Outplay 1v2 Scenarios

Cons

  • Low Mobility
  • Easily Kited
  • Long Early Cooldowns




Summoner Spells

Flash

 
  The most essential summoner spell.
  No reason to NOT pick it.


Teleport

 
  The best summoner spell for top lane.
  You won't really need ignite due to your already amazing damage.






Abilities

  Note: the first three abilities you pick is dependent on the matchup.
  But usually you want to start with:
  Q or E into melee matchups.
  W into ranged matchups.

Passive: Pit Grit

First passive:
 
  Sett's attacks alternate between Left Punch and Right Punch.
  Sett begins attacking with Left Punch. Left Punch is weaker and has less range than Right Punch.
  With Right Punch, Sett gains: 50 attack range, has increased attack speed, and deals bonus physical damage.


Second passive:

  Sett regenerates an additional health per second for every 5% missing health.


Q: Knuckle Down

 
  Sett empowers his next 2 basic attacks, gaining 50 attack range and dealing bonus damage.
  Knuckle Down restarts Sett's basic attack timer.
  If enemy is within a 2000 unit radius, Sett gains 30% bonus movement speed towards them.


W: Haymaker

 
Passive:

  Sett stores post-mitigation damage as grit in his lower bar.
  The most he can store is up to 50% of his max health.
  Grit decays by 30% every second after 4 seconds.

Active:

  Sett gains a shield equal to his grit for 3 seconds.
  The shield starts decaying after 0,75 seconds.

  Sett charges a strike.
  After the charge time - Sett unleashes a blast in a cone area in the target direction.
  The blast deals physical damage in the cone area, but deals true damage in the center.


E: Facebreaker

 

  Sett pulls in enemies from the front and back towards him, dealing physical damage.
  The enemies are slowed by 50% for 0,5 seconds.
  If there is at least 1 enemy on each side, all enemies will be stunned for 1 second.


R: The Show Stopper

 

  Sett dashes with displacement immunity a set distance toward the enemy champion.
  Sett then attaches himself to the enemy, supressing them, and dashes forwards.
  When the dash is completed, Sett slams the enemy into the ground, creating a shockwave in the impact area.

  Enemies within the epicenter take physical damage.
  Enemies outside the epicenter, but within the impact area, take 75% reduced physical damage.
  All enemies hit are slowed by 99% for 1 second.
  The damage scales off the enemy's bonus health.

  The dash will end early if Sett reaches a undashable wall.


Basic Tips:


  Do not use potions before fighting - your passive will heal you, the lower your HP is.
  Q resets your auto-attack timer.
  Your W will not stop if you're CC'd.
  Use your Ultimate on tanks - their bonus health will deal more damage.
